How It Started

FROM SÉTIF
TO ALL OF ALGERIA

Khelil Tech was founded with a simple frustration: quality tech products were either unavailable locally or overpriced by middlemen. We decided to change that.

Starting from a small workshop in Sétif, we built direct relationships with trusted international distributors to offer the real deal — genuine products, verified specs, and prices that respect the customer.

Today, we ship to every corner of Algeria, from the Mediterranean coast to the deep Sahara. Every order is packed with care, and every customer is treated like family.
